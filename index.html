Business Units

The Philippines' oldest and largest conglomerate (founded 1834), Ayala operates across real estate (Ayala Land), banking (BPI), telecoms (Globe Telecom), energy (ACEN), healthcare (AC Health), logistics (AC Logistics), automotive (ACMobility), and electronics manufacturing (IMI). FY2025 core net income hit a record ₱48.3B, driven by BPI and Ayala Land, with AC Health and IMI swinging to profitability. Mitsubishi Corp. acquired a 50% stake in AC Ventures (holding Ayala's GCash stake) for ~₱19B in H2 2025, signalling confidence in Ayala's fintech portfolio. In 2026, the group is pivoting from aggressive expansion to cash flow discipline and balance sheet protection amid global macro headwinds.
